Pending Gene Additions

Here is an Excel spreadsheet with additional embryo mutants not included in the SeedGenes database. Some of these additions represent "Confirmed" or "Not Confirmed" mutants that were added to our datasets after the main database was updated in December, 2010. Others represent "Promising" mutants that require further validation before their identity status can be upgraded. This spreadsheet will be updated on a regular basis to keep track of valuable additions to the cumulative dataset of EMB genes in Arabidopsis.

Pending Alias Additions

Here is an Excel spreadsheet with additional alias symbols for EMB genes of Arabidopsis. These symbols were added to our datasets after the main database was updated in December, 2010. Additional symbols for EMB genes can be found at TAIR (www.arabidopsis.org).
